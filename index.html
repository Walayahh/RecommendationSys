Taqdeer Business Model Summary

Section Details
What do we do? Digital Tipping Platform Enables easy, app-free tipping via QR codes for UAE service workers.
How do we do it? QR-Based Simplicity Uses multilingual QR flows, cloud dashboards, and secure payment systems.
Who do we serve? Target Users Service workers, businesses (salons, cafes), and UAE consumers. Expanding to GCC.
Customer Problem Cash Reliance Customers don’t carry cash; workers can’t easily receive or track tips.
Solution Scan, Tip, Done Instant digital tips—no app required, no login, no complexity.
Pricing & Billing Flat Transaction Fee 0.50 AED per tip; optional paid business features (analytics, branding).
Income Streams Monetization Channels Transaction fees, premium dashboards, NGO/government partnerships.
Customer Reach Strategy Multi-Channel Campaigns Social + influencer ads, physical QR posters, salon/cafe activations.
Referral Strategy Worker & Business Incentives Referral rewards to boost organic user and merchant growth.
Competitive Edge No App Required Seamless, multilingual, and aligned with UAE's digital transformation vision.
Milestone 1 Short-Term KPI 10,000 workers onboarded and 20% conversion to business plans in 6 months.
Milestone 2 Mid-Term KPI Two UAE government partnerships + 50,000 monthly transactions tipped.
